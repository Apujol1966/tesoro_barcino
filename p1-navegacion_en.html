The First Step

Follow the path of my words...

"From the place where your journey begins, you must pass through the fountain of the most innocent with their baskets and continue along the path of the Angel.

Almost at the end of the road, when you don't know which direction to take, leave the women carrying amphorae to your right.

To Barcino, my city, its blood in the form of the liquid of life arrives through its veins made of stone.

There, where its artery begins and ends, where seven figures guarded by the Sun and the Moon rest on the ground, written with them you will find the way forward."

Navigation Clue:

Stand at Plaça Catalunya. Walk down the Portal de l'Àngel avenue to the end.

You will reach the Cathedral square (Plaça Nova). Look for the remains of the Roman Aqueduct attached to the city wall.
